About Me

Hello there! I'm an aspiring web developer and front-end developer based in India.

Having spent a large chunk of my life in technology and engineering, I've finally found my passion at the crossroads of design and technology. I started off as an undergrad in Electronics and Communication Engineering, then worked as an Embedded Developer, and later taught myself web development. The one lesson that stood out throughout this journey was the importance of the end user.

Currently, I spend most of my time learning and designing websites and doing #100DaysOfCode challenge to keep my skills sharp. When I'm not busy, you can find me obsessing over books, anime, food, and listening to music, or working on one of my many hobbies like painting and sketching. I tend to switch things up every few months, so if you want to know what I'm up to, just follow me! :)
